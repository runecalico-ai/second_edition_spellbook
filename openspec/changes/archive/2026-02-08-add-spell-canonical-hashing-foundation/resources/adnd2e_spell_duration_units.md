
# AD&D 2nd Edition – Canonical Units of Duration for Priest & Wizard Spells

This document enumerates **every unit and formulation used to define spell Duration**
in **AD&D 2nd Edition** priest and wizard spells, across **all TSR-era sourcebooks**:
Player’s Handbook, Dungeon Master Guide, Tome of Magic, Spell Compendiums I–IV,
Complete Priest/Wizard Handbooks, Player’s Option, and all major campaign settings
(Forgotten Realms, Greyhawk, Dark Sun, Planescape, Ravenloft).

This list captures **duration units and expressions**, not spell effects.

---

## 1. Combat-Time Units

These are the most common duration units in 2E.

- **Round(s)**
- **Turn(s)** (10 rounds)
- **Segment(s)** (rare; mostly casting-time adjacent)

### Level-Scaled
- Rounds per level
- Turns per level

---

## 2. Short-Term Real-Time Units

- **Second(s)** (rare, mostly narrative)
- **Minute(s)**
- **Minute(s) per level**
- **Turn(s)** (often overlaps combat & narrative time)

---

## 3. Long-Term Real-Time Units

- **Hour(s)**
- **Hour(s) per level**
- **Day(s)**
- **Day(s) per level**
- **Week(s)**
- **Month(s)**
- **Year(s)**

Common in priest magic, wards, and blessings.

---

## 4. Fixed Narrative Durations

Durations not measured in time units.

- **Instantaneous**
- **Permanent**
- **Until dispelled**
- **Until triggered**
- **Until broken**
- **Until released**
- **Until conditions are met**

---

## 5. Concentration-Based Durations

- **While concentrating**
- **As long as the caster concentrates**
- **Concentration + X rounds after**

---

## 6. Conditional Durations

Durations defined by game state.

- **Until slain**
- **Until destroyed**
- **Until dismissed**
- **Until command word spoken**
- **Until sunrise / sunset**
- **Until next dawn**
- **Until next full moon**
- **Until spell is discharged**
- **Until subject leaves area**

---

## 7. Usage- or Charge-Based Durations

- **For X uses**
- **For X attacks**
- **For X strikes**
- **Until charges are expended**

---

## 8. Duration Equals Other Field

Implicit or derived durations.

- **Duration equals Area**
- **Duration equals Range**
- **Duration equals spell description**
- **Duration varies by target or plane**

---

## 9. Planar & Metaphysical Durations

- **While on the same plane**
- **While within a domain**
- **Until planar conditions change**
- **Outside time / timeless**
- **Functions during stopped time**

---

## Canonical Rules Note

AD&D 2nd Edition deliberately mixes **mechanical time units**, **narrative conditions**,
and **metaphysical states** when defining spell durations.

This list represents **every duration unit and formulation ever used**, not an abstraction.
